Safety First

Jump Safely

Your safety is our top priority. All jump areas are supervised by our trained court monitors to ensure rules are followed and everyone has a safe, fun experience.

1. Grip Socks Required

GravityForce grip socks are mandatory on all trampolines to prevent slipping and maintain hygiene.

2. One Per Trampoline

To avoid collisions and double-bouncing, strictly one jumper is allowed per trampoline square.

3. Empty Your Pockets

No phones, keys, or sharp objects allowed on the courts. Lockers are available for rent.
