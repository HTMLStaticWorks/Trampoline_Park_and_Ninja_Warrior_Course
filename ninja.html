Fitness & Growth

Why Train Ninja Obstacles?

Unlike standard weight training, navigating obstacle courses demands full-body functional synchronization. It builds real-world utility strength while improving mental problem-solving speeds under exertion.

  • 🫁 Cardiovascular Endurance: High intensity interval training on consecutive obstacle runs.
  • 🖐️ Superior Grip Strength: Hanging chains, rings, and pegs develop high forearm stamina.
  • 🧠 Mental Focus: Calculating launch timings and body positioning mid-air.
  • 🤸 Flexibility & Joint Health: Dynamic motions build elastic joints and tendon strength.

Safety Guide

Ninja Rules & Guidelines

Required Attire & Grip Shoes ▼

Unlike trampoline zones where grip socks are mandatory, the Ninja Warrior course requires clean, closed-toe athletic sneakers. Sandals, boots, bare feet, or grip socks are strictly prohibited on the obstacle structures to prevent slipping.

One Runner At A Time Rule ▼

To avoid dangerous mid-air collisions, only one active ninja runner is allowed on an individual obstacle lane at any time. Wait until the path is completely clear and the previous jumper has cleared the landing deck before beginning your run.

Chalk & Accessories Policies ▼

Gymnastic chalk is allowed on our elite climbing zones. All ninjas must remove dangling jewelry, keychains, cellphones, and loose clothing items prior to entering the course area to prevent catching on the bars.
