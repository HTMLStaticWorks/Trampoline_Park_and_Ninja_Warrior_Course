General Trampoline Rules ▼

1. No double bouncing: Bouncing on the same trampoline mat with another jumper is strictly prohibited as it can transfer kinetic energy unpredictably.

2. Land on feet: Always try to land on both feet. Never dive headfirst into the trampolines, landing mats, or foam safety zones.

3. Empty pockets: Ensure all keys, jewelry, phone cases, and pocket items are placed inside lockers to prevent injury and screen damages.

Mandatory Grip Socks Policy ▼

To maintain high traction on our trampoline grids and prevent bacteria spreading, all jumpers must wear GravityForce approved safety grip socks. Standard socks or bare feet are not allowed in the trampoline court zones. Grip socks can be purchased for $3 per pair and are yours to keep for future visits.

Age & Height Restrictions ▼

1. Kids Zone: Reserved strictly for children ages 6 and under (maximum height 46 inches) to protect toddlers from larger jumpers.

2. Ninja Warrior Course: Jumpers must be at least 48 inches tall to attempt intermediate obstacles, and 54 inches to attempt the salmon ladder or warped wall.

3. Minors Policy: Jumpers under the age of 16 must have a signed waiver from a parent or legal guardian on file prior to entry.

Digital Waiver Signing Guide ▼

Digital liability waivers must be renewed annually. Signing online prior to arriving at the park is highly recommended to bypass general entry queues. Waivers can be signed from your customer dashboard inside the "Digital Waivers" tab, allowing you to sign for yourself and up to 6 minor children under one account.
