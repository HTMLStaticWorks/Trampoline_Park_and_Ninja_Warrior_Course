Our Mission

Engineered For Fun, Designed For Safety

GravityForce is the premier sports entertainment venue in Action City. Founded in 2021, we set out to build a clean, high-performance center where athletes and families can bounce, train, climb, and celebrate milestones together safely.

Safety First

Our Core Security Pillars

We maintain high safety ratings because we don't cut corners. From engineering to training, safety is in our DNA.

ASTM Standard

All our frames, mats, and safety nets exceed the American Society for Testing and Materials standards to prevent collisions.

CPR Certified Staff

Every single crew supervisor on our jumping floor is fully trained in first-aid, CPR, and accident prevention protocols.

Elite Cleanliness

We disinfect our trampoline zones, foam pits, and Ninja courses every morning using advanced medical-grade spray solutions.

Elite Mentors
